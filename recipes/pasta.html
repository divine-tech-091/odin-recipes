Description

Creamy sauce made from eggs, pecorino romano, guanciale, and pepper.

A good pasta for the body system to stay healthy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basil pesto.
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese,
  • 1 cup reserved pasta water
  • optional cherry tomatoes or pine nuts.

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta: Boil pasta in heavily salted water until al dente.
  2. Save Water: Before draining, reserve about 1 cup of the starchy pasta water.
  3. Combine: Place pasta in a large bowl. Add pesto and 1/2 cup of the pasta water, tossing to combine.
  4. Adjust: Add more pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Serve: Top with extra Parmesan, fresh basil, and a squeeze of lemon juice.
