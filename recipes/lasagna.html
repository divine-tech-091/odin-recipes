Odin Recipes

Description

Delicious Cuisine meal very healthy and fantastic.

A healthy Cuisine that keeps your body strong and energetic

The cuisine relies on traditional cooking methods like smoking, pickling, and curing.

ingredients

  • Fresh fish
  • game(especially reindeer)
  • Root vegetables
  • Wild berries
  • Dense rye bread
  • Creamy salmon soup
  • karelian pastries

Steps

  1. Boil water or fish stock with onions and allspice berries
  2. Add diced, peeled potatoes and cook until soft (about 10 minutes).
  3. Add cubed, fresh salmon and cook for a few more minutes until tender.
  4. Stir in cream and fresh dill before serving with rye bread.
  5. Seasonality: Use berries and mushrooms in autumn, and root vegetables in winter.
  6. Simplicity: Let natural flavors speak for themselves, using minimal, high-quality ingredients.
  7. Foraging: Use wild ingredients like cloudberries, lingonberries, or mushrooms for authentic flavors.
